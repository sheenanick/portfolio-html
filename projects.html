Life Collage

Life Collage is a lifestyle photo app that allows users to take pictures and add them to a collage. There are no tags, captions or comments because we believe the pictures should speak for themselves. Users can also search for other users and view their collages. The other cool feature of Life Collage is that users can "pass" ownership of collages to another user over Bluetooth, so that the new owner can add a picture to the collage, and then pass it on to someone else.

Java, Android SDK

Fitness Tracker

A fitness application for people who go to the gym regularly or are trying to exercise more often. Users can log their exercises to keep track of their daily activity. The main screen is a calendar that highlights which days the user has exercised. Because most people who frequent the gym usually have set routines, there is the option to "save this workout" so that users do not have to manually log the same routine again in the future.

Java, Android SDK

pdxBranch App

An Android application for pdxBranch - a networking site for developers in the Portland, Oregon area. Currently, users can create an account and log in to create posts that are added to their news feed and profile. Posts can include pictures taken on the device. By default, 10 Meetup groups in the tech industry will be listed on the "Groups" screen, but users can also search for Meetup groups by entering key words into the search bar.

Java, Android SDK
